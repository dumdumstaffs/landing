Crypto Currencies

What exactly is a cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrencies (Crypto) are virtual currencies that employ a decentralized network to conduct safe financial transactions. You may trade Crypto CFDs – such as Bitcoin, Ripple XRP, Ethereum, and others – on Global Finance Plus trading platform by speculating on their price fluctuations without actually holding them.

What are the advantages of Crypto CFDs?

  • There is no need for an exchange account or a specialized wallet!
  • Trade-in either way - selling is just as easy as buying.
  • Available seven days a week, twenty-four hours a day!
