Need help?

How do I open an account? +

To open an account with Klips, go to our website klips.com and follow the below steps: 1. Click on “Open an account” on the home page. 2. Fill in the details with your email address and a password you wish and select “Start Trading”. 3. Fill in the mandatory personal details on the registration form. 4. Submit the required documents to verify your account. 5. Once you get verified, you may make your first deposit. 6. You are now ready to start the trading experience.

Is Klips licensed/authorised and regulated? +

Yes. Klips CY Ltd, is a Company authorized and regulated by the Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC) as a Cyprus Investment Firm (“CIF”) with license number 434/23 it operates in accordance with the Markets in Financial Instruments Directive (MiFID) II of the European Union and is a Member of the Investor Compensation Fund (ICF)

Why do I need to verify my account? +

As a fully regulated and licensed financial company, we are at all times obligated and dedicated to complying with all laws and regulations. We are therefore required to "know our clients" and to be able to verify them. Rest assured, your personal details are always kept fully confidential as per our Terms and Conditions and in full compliance with GDPR laws and regulations.

What is a demo account? +

A demo account is a way to practice trading without risking any of your own money, as you'll be using virtual funds. It offers exactly the same tools and features as a live trading account, and you'll be trading under real-market conditions.

How do I make a deposit in my account? +

Yes. You can deposit fiat funds to your Klips account using a credit card. You may also choose another method of depositing from the available payment methods listed after you press the “Deposit” button.

Can I make a wire transfer to make a deposit? +

Yes. You can use a wire to deposit to your Klips Account. You may also choose another method of depositing from the available payment methods listed after you press the “Deposit” button.

Can I talk to a real person of Klips? +

Of course. Klips is available to you on our Chat. You may also communicate with us via email at [email protected].
