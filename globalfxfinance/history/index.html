Our History

Unlike other Forex brokers, Global FX Finance was created by a group of traders and has come a long way from its roots as an educational center to become a major Forex brokerage company that continues to expand rapidly.

The company was founded as an educational center offering courses in the field of technical analyses and financial markets. The launch of brokerage services in 2005 was a natural progression to satisfy the growing need for fair and transparent trading conditions combined with excellent customer support. With us you can be sure of constant support, excellent trading conditions and a trader-oriented approach. We are committed to continue on in this direction and spread our values and benefits to new regions around the world. With the continued growth of the Company, and these guiding principles, we will continue in our quest to become world’s leading Forex brokerage.

2020

  • Launch of Crypto 10 trading account: Account currency (USD, EUR, GBP, RUB, JPY), Min. deposit ($10), Leverage (1:10), Commission (0.25% for half-turn).
  • Deposits and withdrawals via AstroPay Card, a virtual prepaid card. AstroPay issues cards with different prepaid values starting from $10 to $1,000.
  • Launch of a brand-new trading platform: TickTrader. The platform allows clients to trade five of the most popular asset classes (Forex, Stocks, Commodities, Indices, and Cryptocurrencies) via one special ECN TickTrader trading account.
  • New offer for the clients, who registered with Global FX Finance and got Grade 1 verification: 300$ No Deposit Bonus for ECN TickTrader Accounts.
  • New payment methods for Global FX Finance clients worldwide: CASHU, the largest online payment service in the MENA region and Local Bank Transfers including such methods as Rapipago (Argentina), Boleto Bancário (Brazil) and Oxxo (Mexico).
  • One more Index CFD for trading: Hong Kong 50, which tracks the daily price changes of the largest companies listed on the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong (SEHK).
  • The commissions for indices are calculated in percent from the transaction volume.
  • Implementation of PAMM account drawdown limit. This mechanism allows reducing losses of Subscribers' funds in case of unprofitable trading on a PAMM account.
  • 'No commission deposits' promotion has been lasting for 9 months. All the actual payment commissions can be found on the 'Fees' page.

2019

  • Launch of PAMM ECN accounts on the MetaTrader 5 Platform.
  • Widgets with information about a trading account in Global FX Finance Analytics.
  • Development of the verification procedure helping avoid fraud registrations and violations. New conditions for receiving Welcome bonus and No deposit bonus for STP.
  • Direct deposits/ transfers/ withdrawals to/from a trading account in My Global FX Finance: thus Internal transfer is performed automatically by the system.
  • 'No commission deposits' promotion having lasted for 5 months: Global FX Finance clients were able to deposit their accounts via any payment system available (except for Bank Wire Transfer) without the cost of fees charged by the payment systems.
  • Special offers for new and existing clients like Black Friday and New Year 0% ECN commissions.

2018

  • Launched MetaTrader 5 for ECN accounts.
  • Introduced multi-factor authentication (via Google Authenticator, SMS, Email or backup codes) and improved the account verification procedure.
  • Received a certificate for high-quality order execution from VerifyMyTrade and the Financial Commission.
  • Added new cryptocurrency pairs with Bitcoin Cash, Ethereum, Ripple, EOS, Monero, NEO, IOTA, OMNI, etc. and now offers the total of 43 currency pairs on Crypto accounts.
  • Reduced SWAP on Crypto accounts down to 10%.
  • Introduced new bank transfer deposit options for Africa and Asia, as well as deposits and withdrawals in Emercoin.

2017

  • The launch of PAMM-Crypto accounts, so that PAMM-Masters and their Followers can benefit from investing into the growing and highly liquid crypto market.

  • Deposits and withdrawals via Alipay, WeChat Pay and ChinaUnion Pay - Asia's leading mobile payment solutions.
