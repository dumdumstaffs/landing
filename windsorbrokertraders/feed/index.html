Windsor Brokers launches its new copy trading platform WB Copy Trading

Windsor Brokers introduces WB Copy Trading, a new and innovative platform that allows traders to fast-track their trading journey by copying the moves of the strategy providers.

With WB Copy Trading, investors can maximize their potential profits by copying the trades of strategy providers around the world. Signing up for WB Copy Trading takes just a few clicks. From there, clients can hand-pick a top trader to copy and set up their risk management options. Once they activate the subscription, the top trader’s positions will be automatically reflected in their accounts.

“WB Copy Trading is designed to provide investors with the tools they need to trade the markets,” says Johny Abuaitah, CEO of Windsor Brokers. “We believe that traders should have access to the best strategies and tools to achieve their goals, and that’s what we’re offering.”

WB Copy Trading is transparent, customizable, and automatic. Traders can view strategy providers’ history and performance, set their own copy rules, and sit back while the top traders take care of the trades. Most importantly, traders only pay a performance fee if the strategy increases their earnings.

With WB Copy Trading, traders are given the gift of convenience and flexibility.

Windsor Brokers Organizes a Donation Drive for Earthquake Victims in Turkey and Syria

Countless bags were collected on Monday morning at Windsor Broker headquarters in Limassol. Many more will be gathered throughout this week before they are shipped to their final destination in Turkey and Syria with the help of the Limassol Municipality in collaboration with the EU Civil Protection, Red Cross and Red Crescent.

Windsor Brokers Proud Major Sponsors of 'Sing Together for Alma'

As part of our social responsibility, Windsor Brokers were the proud major sponsors of a very special charity event led by Limassol Lions Club called, ‘Sing Together for Alma,’ which took place on November 6th. This event bought local businesses and individuals together in an afternoon of live music and entertainment in the local town square of Limassol, Cyprus. Over the last 52 years, the Limassol Lions Club has been built on a group of selfless, loving individuals who voluntarily serve members of society in need, that organise activities and events which raise money for various causes. This particular event was held to purchase equipment for the Children’s Skills Development Center ‘To Alma’, a program of the Theotokos Foundation. They offer early childhood intervention services to families with children up to 6 years of age with development difficulties. It is a great pleasure to be part of such local charitable events and give back to our local community, we hope to inspire as many of you as possible to be involved with such wonderful organisations and give your local community a helping hand where you can.
