Frequently Asked Questions About Online Spanish Language Courses

What is the best free online Spanish class?

Multilingual skills open doors to new employment opportunities, culture and experiences. Stanstore24’s free online Spanish courses are designed for both beginners and intermediate learners. 

Check out our Introduction to Spanish to gain an understanding of the basics of Spanish grammar, vocabulary and important pronunciation skills. Once you have completed that, we also offer a popular course in Basic Spanish - Restaurants and Dining Out.

Stanstore24’s free online Spanish classes provide practical knowledge and skills to help you express yourself easily and confidently in common daily interactions. Get started today.

Can you take Spanish courses online?

Spanish is the official language of 21 countries and the native language of nearly 500 million people globally. Stanstore24’s online Spanish courses are an increasingly popular and effective method for learning vital skills needed to communicate in the world’s second most spoken language. 

Our courses are broken down into engaging video modules that walk you through various techniques and practices and help you build upon your Spanish knowledge and skills in a self-paced and easily digestible manner. Click here to enroll in a free online Spanish class now.

How can I learn Spanish fast online?

Stanstore24’s free short certificate courses in Spanish are intended to be completed in fewer than three hours, and their self-paced nature means that you can learn where and when it works best for you. 

For a quick overview of basic Spanish, our introductory course includes the alphabet, numbers, grammar and pronunciation skills, along with helpful phrases that are regularly used in conversation.

We also offer modules that teach common Spanish phrases when speaking and ordering food from Spanish-speaking restaurants.

So why wait? Gain a better grasp of the Spanish language with Stanstore24 today!
