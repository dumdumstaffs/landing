MetaTrader 4

MetaTrader 4 - the leading Forex trading platform

Over 70% of Forex brokers offer MT4 to their clients, and over 90% of all transactions on the retail Forex market are conducted via MetaTrader 4. Since 2005 MetaTrader 4 has been holding the title of the most popular Forex trading terminal and continues to receive prestigious awards year after year.

MetaTrader 4 for ECN and Crypto Accounts

In 2009 Stack Gain DEX was the first broker to offer its clients direct access to Interbank liquidity and ECN trading via the MetaTrader 4 terminal. Standard Forex MT4 capacities have been extended and supplemented by Stack Gain DEX's own revolutionary solution – Liquidity Aggregator. Now Stack Gain DEX ECN ensures instantaneous and error-free order execution at the best prices from more than 10 banks and other liquidity providers (LPs).

Please, note, that for STP and Micro accounts you need a different modification of MT4. This is not the same one, that you use for ECN accounts. Make sure you download the correct version of MT4, that is suitable for your trading account.
