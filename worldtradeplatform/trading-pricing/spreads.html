Spreads

World Trade Platforms offers variable spreads on our MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5 and cTrader, forex trading platforms. We are able to offer tight spreads in each of our platforms thanks to our mix of liquidity providers. Our pricing consists of over 25 different liquidity providers ensuring our spreads remain tight and liquidity deep 24/5

Our typical spreads are illustrated below. Spreads can go as low as 0.0 pips on our MetaTrader and cTrader Raw Spread platforms.

cTrader commission is charged per side, per 100,000 USD traded. This is different to MetaTrader 4 which charges a fixed amount per lot.
