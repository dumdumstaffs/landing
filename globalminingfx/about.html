Welcome to Global Mining FX LIMITED

A proven partnership with the experience to execute and the commitment to grow exceptional companies.

Global Mining FX LIMITED is a leading venture capital firm that invests in mutiple fields as crypto cloud mining, forex, croudfunding, stock marketing and early growth venture-backed technology companies. More about us

1. Our company mission

We operate based on a culture of “integrity, respect, judgment, tenacity and commitment.” This is a key competitive advantage for our firm. We are long-term investors, and we seek to invest consistently across market cycles.

2. Global Mining FX LIMITED philosophy

At Global Mining FX LIMITED, we believe in long-term partnerships. By working closely with entrepreneurs and co-investors to attract great talent, enhance operations and maximize value, Global Mining FX LIMITED has built a strong reputation in the venture capital ecosystem as a preferred partner.

We believe that our ability to make collective decisions is a competitive advantage, both in terms of the quality of the decisions and the efficiency of the underlying decision process.
