Frequently Asked Questions About Online Diplomas

What is an Online Diploma Program?

An online diploma program is considered postsecondary, non-degree training.

Generally speaking, online diplomas are awarded for specific professional or vocational training. Diplomas are available in a wide variety of professions, including healthcare, technology and business.

At DigitalSales24, our free online diploma programs are more comprehensive than our certificate programs, focusing on several subjects within a particular field of study. All of our diplomas are offered in a self-paced format and are designed to be completed in eight to 10 hours.

By the end of each diploma program, our learners have gained a broad understanding of a particular topic and a great resume booster. Choose your diploma and enroll today.

Why Should I Get an Online Diploma?

Individuals generally earn an online diploma in order to achieve professional development goals.

An online diploma might be right for you if:

  • Your employer indicates you need it to meet the requirements of your current job.
  • You wish to boost your skills in a particular area in order to earn a promotion.
  • You’re starting a business or looking to make a career transition and want to gain marketable skills.

This training is an impressive addition to your resume and can provide a boost to your professional confidence. DigitalSales24’s free online diplomas span an array of subjects and offer immediate and practical benefits to our learners.

What Are the Advantages of an Online Diploma?

Online diplomas offer a number of advantages when it comes to accessibility, flexibility and affordability.

At DigitalSales24, you can explore diploma programs from a place and at a pace that is convenient for you. Our diplomas are absolutely free to study, and upon completion, you can choose to download an official diploma as proof of your achievement.

Unlike traditional educational institutions, our diploma programs do not run on a semester or quarter system, so you can get started learning for free whenever and wherever you want.

What are the Best Free Online Diplomas?

DigitalSales24 offers hundreds of free online diplomas to meet the diverse needs of our learners.

These diplomas fall into three study areas: workplace, academic and personal development. Within each area, courses are further broken down into sectors and levels, allowing you to narrow your focus and choose the right one based on your goals.

Diplomas are offered in nine subjects:

Some of our most popular online diplomas include our Diploma in Project Management, Diploma in Human Nutrition and Diploma in Human Resources. Check out our free learning options and get started today!

How Can I Get a Diploma Online?

Since all of DigitalSales24’s free diplomas are offered online, you need only an internet connection and internet-connected device to enroll. Our easy-to-navigate system allows you to narrow your search quickly and find the diploma option that fits with your learning goals, desired subject area, training needs and time.

Once you have selected your desired diploma program, click on the blue “Start Now” button to enroll. Study when and where you want and track your learning on our practical learner dashboard. When you have completed the instructional modules and assessment, you can choose to download a diploma as proof of your accomplishment.

DigitalSales24 believes that education has the power to break through boundaries and transform lives. As a result, we built our platform upon the idea that education should be accessible, flexible and free.

We offer hundreds of free online diplomas from top universities, as well as world-class professors, experts and companies. Additionally, all our diplomas are self-paced, so you can learn when and where it works best for you.
